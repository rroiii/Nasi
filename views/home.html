Nourishment Alert and Surveillance Interface

Food security in West Java, Indonesia, becomes a critical issue amid rapid population growth and intensive agricultural land conversion. Utilizing deep learning and Artificial Neural Network (ANN) technologies, this analysis models the change in land cover from 2020 to 2032 and predicts population growth in the same period. The aim is to assist in ensuring future food security through strategies that take into account population dynamics and changes in agricultural land area, as well as providing a basis for effective policies in maintaining sustainable food supply in the region.

Indoesia Emas 2045

One of the pillars is sustainable economic development. There are several indicators that must be realized to establish this pillar, one of which is the consolidation of food security and the improvement of the welfare of farmers.

Deep Learning

The integration of deep learning and GIS analysis yields an assessment of land cover changes and population expansion in West Java, providing recommendations to policymakers in preventing food crises.

WebGIS

This WebGIS serves as an information platform and collaboration hub between various parties to achieve food security in West Java, in line with the realization of Indonesia Emas Vision 2045.
